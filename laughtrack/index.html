Frequently Asked Questions

Why? For fun! TV shows rarely use laugh tracks or canned laughter these days. This app lets us relive the good old days. And maybe feel that those weren't the days, after all.

How? Built in HTML/JS with the AudioContext API.

Why does it need access to my microphone? To hear the funny jokes.

Is the voice data recorded or sent anywhere? All processing happens on the phone. No data is saved. No data is stored. No data is sent anywhere. Your best jokes are completely safe with you.

Does the app actually understand the jokes? There is no voice recognition processing done to the sound. The app is very simple: it will laugh any time there is a bit of silence (after a bit of non-silence).
